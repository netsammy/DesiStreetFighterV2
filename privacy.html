Desi Street Brawler Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Desi Street Brawler is a fighting game. This policy explains what data is stored or processed when you use the app.

Data Stored On Your Device

The game stores local settings and gameplay data on your device, such as audio settings, touch-control settings, quality settings, CPU/player preferences, and coins earned from rewarded ads. This data stays on your device and is not sent to a developer-operated server.

Ads

The app uses Google AdMob to show optional rewarded ads and post-match ads. Google AdMob may collect or process data such as device identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, and advertising-related signals according to Google's policies and your device settings.

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Internet Access

Internet access is used for ads and related Google services. The core game can run without an internet connection, but ads and ad rewards may not be available while offline.

Children

Desi Street Brawler is intended for a general game audience and is not directed specifically to young children. Parents and guardians should review the app's store rating and ad behavior before allowing children to play.
